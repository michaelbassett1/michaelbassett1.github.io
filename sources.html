Data Sources

There were two primary data sources used while completing this project. The first was The World Bank, an organization known for its focus on international development, poverty and sustainability. Their GDP, population and military spending figures were utilized. The second source was the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development, or OECD. The OECD aims to stimulate economic progress and world trade. Their healthcare and education figures were included in my analysis.

Both groups offer a myriad of economic indicators and other data that are free to the public:
