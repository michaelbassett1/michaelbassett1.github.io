Introduction

On this webpage, I have laid out a variety of data visualizations pertaining to education, healthcare, and military spending around the world.

In the Visualizations tab, several unique charts have been created that look at:

  • Education, Healthcare, and Military Spending over time
  • Spending data compared to Gross Domestic Product (GDP)
  • Education and Healthcare Spending in relation to Military Spending
  • Per Capita Comparisons
  • Changes in Education and Healthcare Spending over time
